HELLO!

My name is K.K. Rebecca Lai. I love making maps, especially of my adventures.

The Hong Kong Four Trails has inspired a lot of my work. I backpacked across the trails in the Summer of 2022. I also did a 100-km ultra marathon on the MacLehose Trail in 2024. These were among the most memorable things I've done in my life.

I moved to Vancouver in recent years and fell in love with bouldering. Hence my latest map of the top 100 boulder problems in Squamish.

If any of my work interests you, I would love to chat! You can find me at kkrebeccalai at gmail.
